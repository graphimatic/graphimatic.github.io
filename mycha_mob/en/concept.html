The world of plants
according to My Cha

My Cha is inspired by the traditional herbalist shops of old with their wonderful mix of scents, by the medicinal plants from our gardens, and a little french gourmandise…
My Cha brings you a range of herbal teas that suit your physical and emotional needs throughout the seasons.
My Cha also loves to prepare personalised herbal teas for a gourmet restaurant, luxury hotel, spa, or for special events.
The plants for the creation of the teas are aromatic and medicinal plants from controlled origins and are chosen for their virtues and qualities, My Cha herbal teas are free of caffeine and theine.
