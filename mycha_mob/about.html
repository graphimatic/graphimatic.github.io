The world of plants according to My Cha

the magical new world of herbs

My Cha is inspired by the traditional herbalist shops with their wonderful mix of scents, by the medicinal plants from our gardens, and a little french gourmandise…
We offer you a variety of herbal teas to meet your physical and emotional needs throughout the seasons.
My Cha also loves to prepare personalised herbal teas for a gourmet restaurant, luxury hotel, spa, or for special events.
The plants for the creation of the teas are aromatic and medicinal plants from controlled origins and are chosen for their virtues and qualities, My Cha herbal teas are free of caffeine and theine.

100% natural!

No artificial flavouring... The plants for the creation of the teas are aromatic and medicinal plants from controlled origins and are chosen for their virtues and qualities
No artificial colours... The Blue of Blue moon? The Pink of Madame a besoin d’energie? The flower in the teas produce these colours. Nature surprises and amuses us!
No preservatives... In their hermetically sealed packs protected from air and light, the teas remain fresh.

My Cha's creator

Laetitia Arrighi de Casanova is the soul of My Cha.


She lives and works in Lisbon and maintains strong ties to Paris.

Fascinated by plants in all their forms, she combines consulting with creating My Cha herbal teas. She doesn't miss an opportunity to travel around the world to inspiring and exotic locations and brings newly found influences to My Cha's unique herb blends.
